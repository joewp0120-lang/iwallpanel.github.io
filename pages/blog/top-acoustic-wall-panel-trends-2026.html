Top Acoustic Wall Panel Trends for 2026: Slat & Hexagon Designs

Published: January 7, 2026 ¬∑ Category: Industry Trends

As we move into 2026, the demand for acoustic solutions that do not compromise on aesthetics is at an all-time high. Architects and interior designers are increasingly looking for materials that offer both sound control and visual impact.

1. The Rise of Wood Slat Panels

Wood slat acoustic panels continue to dominate the market. The combination of natural wood veneer with a sound-absorbing PET felt backing offers a timeless look that fits both modern and traditional interiors. Expect to see more variations in wood species and slat widths.

2. Hexagon & Geometric Patterns

Modular design is key. Hexagon PET panels allow for creative freedom, enabling designers to create unique wall art that also functions as acoustic treatment. Mix-and-match colors are becoming popular for dynamic office spaces.

3. Sustainability & Recycled Materials

Eco-friendliness is no longer optional. Clients are demanding transparency regarding material origins. Our panels are made with up to 60% recycled PET plastic bottles, meeting the highest standards for green building certifications.
