Integrated Wall Panels Installation Guide

December 18, 2025 ¬∑ Installation ¬∑ Guide

Tools Checklist

  • Measuring tape, level, pencil
  • Saw or cutter, drill, screwdriver
  • Adhesive and screws as required
  • Protective gloves and eyewear

Step-by-Step Installation

  • Measure and mark the layout on the wall
  • Cut panels to fit with accurate edges
  • Mount using screws or adhesive in sequence
  • Check level and alignment, then finish edges

Pro Tips

  • Plan joints and corner trims for clean lines
  • Use templates for repeating patterns like slats
  • Leave expansion gaps where necessary
