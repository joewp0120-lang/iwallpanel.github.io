The MEA Challenge: Why "Standard" WPC Fails

In markets like Saudi Arabia, the UAE, Nigeria, and Kenya, WPC panels face three silent killers:

  • Thermal Expansion: Intense heat causes low-grade panels to buckle and warp.
  • UV Degradation: High solar radiation bleaches colors within months if not properly stabilized.
  • Regulatory Hurdles: Navigating SABER (Saudi Arabia) or SONCAP (Nigeria) certifications can delay a project for weeks.
