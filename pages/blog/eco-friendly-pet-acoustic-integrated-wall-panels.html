What Are Integrated Wall Panels?

Integrated wall panels combine structure, finish, and acoustic performance into a unified solution. With PET materials, they achieve lightweight construction, stable dimensions, and high sound absorption.

Eco-friendly PET Advantages

  • Recycled materials with low VOC emissions
  • Consistent acoustic performance with NRC up to 0.8+
  • Rich finishes: hexagon, slat, woodgrain, marble
  • Fast installation and easy maintenance

Use Cases

Office meeting rooms, hotel lobbies, home theaters, restaurants, and home offices benefit from improved sound quality and design aesthetics.
