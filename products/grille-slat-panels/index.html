Grille / Slat Panels

Combine the warmth of natural wood with the acoustic performance of PET felt. Our slat panels transform any space into a modern, quiet sanctuary.

Key Features

  • Structure: Wood Veneer + MDF Core + PET Felt Backing
  • Wood Species: Oak, Walnut, Ash, Teak, Black
  • Acoustic Performance: High sound absorption coefficients
  • Installation: Easy screw or glue installation
  • Application: Living rooms, offices, hotel lobbies, recording studios
  • Price: $2-4.5/CBM (MOQ 100 CBM)
  • Lead Time: 3-7 Days

Technical Specifications

Material Composition Wood veneer slats over an MDF core with acoustic PET felt backing (project-based configurations for wholesale supply).
Common Finish Options Oak, walnut, ash, teak, black (additional veneer selections available for OEM/ODM orders).
Thickness / Panel Build Multiple build-ups supported; we recommend confirming the acoustic target and wall substrate to finalize the specification.
Fire Retardant Level Fire-retardant options available; test documentation can be provided based on destination market requirements.
Moisture & Durability Designed for long-term interior performance; we can recommend suitable configurations for higher-humidity environments upon request.
Environmental & Compliance Project documentation support available for global procurement (spec sheets, packing list, and required declarations upon request).
Wholesale Customization OEM/ODM branding, packaging, and mixed-item consolidation for container shipments.

Installation & Maintenance

  • Quick installation: Install by screws or adhesive depending on the wall substrate and project requirements.
  • Clean alignment: Consistent slat spacing helps installers achieve straight lines and a premium finish.
  • Low maintenance: Routine dusting and light wiping keep the surface looking new—no frequent refinishing.
  • Acoustic comfort: PET felt backing improves room acoustics, reducing echo in offices, hotels, and studios.
  • Wholesale-ready: Durable packing and clear labeling support distributor warehousing and jobsite delivery.
